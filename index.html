Frequently Asked Questions

What types of glass does Glassquick offer?

Glassquick offers toughened glass, frosted glass, laminated glass, reflective glass, tinted glass, and decorative glass for both residential and commercial applications. Each type is available in custom sizes and finishes.

Can I get customised glass designs for my home?

Yes. All Glassquick products are available in custom sizes, shapes, and finishes. Share your room dimensions and design preferences and our team will provide a tailored solution and quote.

Is toughened glass safe for shower enclosures?

Yes. Toughened (tempered) glass is the industry standard for shower enclosures. It is 4–5 times stronger than ordinary glass. If it does break, it shatters into small blunt fragments rather than sharp shards, significantly reducing injury risk.

How long does professional glass installation take?

Installation time varies by product type. A standard shower partition takes 4–6 hours. A full-room glass partition system may take 1–2 days. Our team provides a specific timeline at the time of measurement and booking.

Do you offer a warranty on glass products?

Yes. All Glassquick products come with a manufacturer warranty. The warranty period varies by product — typically 1 year on hardware and fittings and 5 years on glass panels. Contact us for product-specific warranty details.
