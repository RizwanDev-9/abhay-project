Exchange policy

Our exchange policy

If there's a manufacturing defect or installation issue — we take full responsibility and replace it. No hassle.

Defects fully covered Report within 24 hours Free replacement & install

Important — 24 hour reporting window

Any defect or issue must be reported within 24 hours of installation. After this window, exchange requests will not be accepted. Please inspect the glass carefully on installation day.

When exchange applies

Only two scenarios are covered — we keep it honest and clear

Exchange eligible ✓

Manufacturing defect — cracks, bubbles, surface damage, coating failure present in the glass itself
Installation defect — fitting issue, glass damaged during our installation process, or structural problem caused by our team

Not eligible ✗

Wrong size due to customer's own measurement error
Damage after installation caused by customer or third party
Change of mind or style preference after delivery
Reported after 24 hours of installation

Why customer measurement errors aren't covered: We do a 2-level measurement process — our technician visits your site and you approve final measurements before production begins. This eliminates sizing errors on our end, and ensures both sides are protected.

How to raise an exchange

Act on the same day — 24 hours goes fast

On installation day

Inspect before our team leaves

Check the glass thoroughly while our installation team is still at your site. Look for any surface defects, cracks, chips, or fitting issues before signing off.

Do not confirm the order if you see any issue — raise it on the spot with the installer.

Step 1 — Within 24 hours

Photograph the defect clearly

Take clear photos or a short video of the defect or installation issue. Show the full panel and a close-up of the problem area.

Good-quality photos speed up review significantly — make sure the lighting is clear.

Step 2 — Within 24 hours

Report via WhatsApp or call

Send your order number, photos, and a brief description to us on WhatsApp or call our team. This must be done within 24 hours of installation.

WhatsApp is fastest — photos + message in one go. We respond the same day.

Step 3

We review & confirm eligibility

Our team reviews your photos and order details. If the exchange is approved, we confirm within the same day and schedule replacement production and delivery.

Step 4

Replacement delivered & installed — free

We deliver the replacement glass and install it at no extra cost. Our team removes the defective piece, fits the new one, and cleans up.

Step 5

Your confirmation closes the exchange

Once you're fully satisfied with the replacement, you confirm and the exchange is complete. We don't close it until you're happy.
