GlassQuick is India's leading digital-first glass brand, providing end-to-end solutions for mirrors, shower partitions, windows, and more using laser measurement technology.
We currently operate in Mumbai, Pune, Thane, Navi Mumbai, Noida, and Gurgaon. We are rapidly expanding to other cities across India.
We use professional-grade laser measurement tools and proprietary software to ensure accuracy within 1mm, significantly reducing errors compared to traditional tape measurements.
Site visit fees may apply depending on your location and project size. However, this fee is often adjusted against your final order value.
All installations are carried out by our own team of certified GlassQuick specialists. We do not outsource installations to third-party contractors to maintain quality.
Our 1-year warranty covers manufacturing defects and installation issues. It does not cover accidental breakage or damage caused by improper maintenance.
Orders can be cancelled before they go into processing. Once the glass is cut to your custom measurements, cancellations are not possible. Please refer to our Cancellation Policy for details.
