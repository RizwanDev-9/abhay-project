Shipping & Logistics Policy

We handle your fragile products with extreme care. Learn about our delivery timelines, handling procedures, and safety protocols.

1. Delivery Timeline

Our standard delivery timeline depends on the product type and customization level:

  • Standard Mirrors: 3-5 business days.
  • Custom Cut Glass: 7-10 business days.
  • Shower Partitions & Large Projects: 10-14 business days.

Note: Timelines may vary based on your location and specific order requirements.

2. Shipping Charges

Shipping charges are calculated based on the order value and delivery distance:

  • Orders above ₹5,000: Free delivery within city limits.
  • Orders below ₹5,000: A nominal delivery fee of ₹250 - ₹500 applies.
  • Outstation Deliveries: Calculated based on actual freight costs.

3. Handling & Safety

Glass is fragile, and we take its safety seriously:

  • Professional Packaging: Every product is packed using specialized edge protectors, foam, and wooden crates where necessary.
  • Specialized Vehicles: We use dedicated vehicles equipped with glass racks to prevent movement and vibration during transit.
  • Trained Personnel: Only trained GlassQuick handlers manage the loading and unloading of your orders.

4. Order Tracking

Once your order is out for delivery, you will receive an SMS and Email notification with the contact details of the delivery partner. You can also track your status on the GlassQuick app.

5. Inspection at Delivery

We require the customer or an authorized representative to inspect the product upon delivery. Any visible damage or defects must be reported immediately to the delivery team and noted on the delivery receipt.
