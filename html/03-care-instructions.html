Product Care Guide

Mirrors

  • Use microfiber cloth only
  • Use ammonia-free cleaner
  • Avoid water on edges
  • No rough scrubbers

Shower Glass

  • Wipe dry after use
  • Clean weekly with mild soap
  • Prevent water stains
  • Keep channels clean

Toughened Glass

  • Use soft cloth
  • Avoid corner impact
  • No sharp objects
  • Check fittings regularly

Railings & Partitions

  • Clean glass & hardware
  • Check loose fittings
  • Keep tracks dust free
  • Lubricate hinges if needed

Cleaning Do's & Don'ts

Do's

  • Use microfiber cloth
  • Use mild cleaner
  • Clean regularly
  • Dry after cleaning

Don'ts

  • No steel wool
  • No acid or bleach
  • No blade scraping
  • No hitting edges

5 Minute Weekly Care Routine

1

Remove dust from surface using a dry microfiber cloth

2

Spray a non-ammonia based glass cleaner lightly

3

Wipe in a circular motion with a clean cloth

4

Dry immediately with a fresh soft cloth to prevent streaks

5

Inspect fittings and hinges for any loose screws

Important: Damage caused by misuse, impact, harsh chemicals, or improper handling may not be covered under warranty.
