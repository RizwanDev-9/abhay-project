How bulk orders work

A straightforward process from enquiry to installation

Step 1

Share your requirements

Tell us the quantity, glass type, approximate sizes, and project timeline. A rough estimate is enough to get started.

Step 2

Receive a custom quote

We send you a detailed quote with bulk pricing applied. No hidden charges — what you see is what you pay.

Step 3

Technician site visit

Our measurement engineer visits your site, takes precise measurements for every piece, and logs them directly into our system.

Step 4

We share the final measurements with you. Once you confirm, glass cutting and processing begins — priority handled.

Step 5

Order delivery

Your order is delivered to your site. We coordinate with your team to ensure a smooth handover.

Step 6

Order closed on your sign-off

Once you're fully satisfied, you confirm and the order is officially closed. Every piece, every unit — signed off by you.
