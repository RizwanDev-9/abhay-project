When should you use Toughened Glass?

Toughened (tempered) glass is mandatory for applications where safety is a concern. It is 5x stronger than regular glass and shatters into small, blunt granules instead of sharp shards.

  • Mandatory for all Shower Enclosures
  • Required for glass below 3 feet from floor
  • Essential for Railings and Balconies
  • Best for Table Tops and Desk protection
