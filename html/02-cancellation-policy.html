Cancellation policy

Our cancellation policy

We keep it simple and transparent — your ability to cancel depends on where your order is in the process.

Cancellation window by order stage

The earlier you cancel, the easier it is for both sides

Stage 1 — Order placed

Free cancellation

Your order is placed and basic measurements have been shared. No site visit has happened yet, no production started.

Full refund. You can cancel freely at this stage — just contact us before the site visit is scheduled.

Stage 2 — After site visit

Partial refund

Our technician has visited your site, taken measurements, and logged them into our system. Time and resources have been used.

Partial refund. A site visit charge will be deducted. Remaining amount refunded within 5–7 working days.

Stage 3 — Production started

No cancellation

You have approved the measurements and glass cutting / processing has begun. Material has been used and cannot be reversed.

No refund once production begins. This is why we always take your approval before starting — to protect both sides.

Stage 4 — After delivery & installation

Order closed

Glass has been delivered and installed by our team. You have confirmed satisfaction and the order is closed.

Order is closed. If there's a defect or fitting issue, please contact us — we handle those separately under our quality commitment.

Stage 5 — Customer confirmation

Order complete

You've confirmed everything is perfect. The order is officially complete — no further changes can be made.

Why we take your approval

We never start cutting glass without your sign-off on measurements — this protects you from any errors.

Refund timeline

Eligible refunds are processed within 5–7 working days of cancellation confirmation.

Custom orders

Custom-cut or special-spec orders cannot be cancelled once production begins — no exceptions.

Installation issues

If there's a defect or fitting problem after installation, contact us — this is handled separately.
