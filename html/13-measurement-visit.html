How it works

From order to installation — here's the complete journey

Step 1

Place your order

Share basic measurements when placing the order. This helps us prepare — exact dimensions come later.

Step 2

Technician site visit

Our measurement engineer visits your shipping address, takes full on-site measurements, and records them directly in our ERP system.

Step 3

Your final approval

We share the recorded measurements with you for confirmation. Once you approve, glass processing begins — no surprises.

Step 4

Delivery & professional installation

Your glass is delivered and our team handles full installation. We take complete responsibility — from delivery to fitting.

Step 5

Order closed on your confirmation

Once you're fully satisfied after installation, you confirm and the order is officially closed. Your sign-off is the only finish line.

Why two measurements? Site conditions — walls, frames, openings — often differ slightly from initial estimates. Our technician visit eliminates guesswork and prevents costly remakes.
