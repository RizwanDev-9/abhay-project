Data Protection

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. Learn how GlassQuick collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you request a quote, book a site visit, or contact our support team. This includes:

  • Contact Data: Name, email address, phone number, and delivery address.
  • Project Data: Site measurements, photos of installation areas, and product preferences.
  • Payment Data: Billing information and transaction history (we do not store credit card numbers on our servers).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Schedule and perform site measurement visits.
  • Process and fulfill your custom glass orders.
  • Send order updates and service notifications.
  • Improve our products and website user experience.
  • Provide customer support and resolve any issues.

3. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration. Our website uses SSL encryption for all data transmissions.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information with trusted third-party partners who assist us in operating our business, such as delivery partners and payment processors, under strict confidentiality agreements.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal information stored with us. You can request this by contacting our privacy team at privacy@glassquick.com.
